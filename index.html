Projects

WAKEY: Agent-Mediated Parent-Child Communication for Better Morning Routines

  • Product Lead
  • 9 team members
  • 6 Honors and Awards
  • 2 Publications

Found that parents are more likely to experience communication conflict with preschool children (3-6 years old) on school mornings. In consultation with domain professionals and families, we designed WAKEY, a technology-based approach that helps parents use better communication strategies to teach preschool children to carry out their morning routines. Following the intervention with WAKEY, parents reported significantly reduced levels of frustration during morning routines and greater independent behavior by children.

Yogee: The Service of Recording Travel

  • Team Leader
  • 7 team members

Travelers often spend a lot of time to sort out photos and logs after travel. And sometimes because of busy work, their memories gradually fade away. Therefore, we designed YOGEE, an app that is combined with Moves, Dropbox and Log Function, so that users can easily record and share their life.

TRAINSEAT: Taiwan Railway booking system - direct and segmented ticket tracing service

  • Team Leader
  • 8 team members

As the official Taiwan Railway system is only provided by the direct train booking method, if the direct tickets are sold out, the user can only find whether there are segmented tickets one by one, resulting in the inconvenience of the purchase. Therefore, We developed TRAINSEAT, a service with the “serial” tickets searching function and the ticket tracing function to improve the user experience of booking tickets.
